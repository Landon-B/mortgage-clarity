Understand Your Options

What Is Mortgage Protection Insurance?

A straightforward explanation — so you can decide what's right for your family.

Mortgage protection insurance is a policy designed to pay off some or all of your remaining mortgage balance if you pass away, become critically ill, or are unable to work due to disability. It ensures your family can stay in their home during the most difficult moments of their lives.

Unlike private mortgage insurance (PMI), which protects the lender, mortgage protection insurance protects you and your family. The benefit goes directly to your loved ones or your mortgage servicer — keeping your home secure regardless of what happens.

Is this right for me?

This coverage may be a good fit if you can say yes to most of these:

  • You currently have a mortgage on your home
  • Your spouse, children, or dependents rely on your income
  • Your family would struggle with mortgage payments if you couldn't work
  • You want a safety net beyond a standard life insurance policy
  • You'd feel more secure knowing your home is protected

Who This Is Not For

We believe in honesty. Mortgage protection isn't the right move for everyone, and that's okay.

  • You rent your home and don't have a mortgage
  • You have no dependents and no one relies on your income
  • You already have comprehensive life and disability coverage that fully accounts for your mortgage
  • You're looking for the absolute cheapest option without understanding fit
Why are we telling you this? Because our goal is to help the right people find the right coverage — not to sell a policy to everyone. If this doesn't describe you, that's a good thing. You may already be covered.
